📋 Introduction

Welcome to DukanBuddy (“we”, “our”, or “us”), a business management application developed by Smart Solutions for small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in Pakistan. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (com.smartsolutions.dukankhata).

By using DukanBuddy, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Category Examples Purpose
Account Information Email address, password (hashed) Admin account creation and login
Business Information Business name, store address, tax number, logo Generating invoices and store listings
Customer Data Customer name, phone number, email, balance Managing customer accounts and ledgers
Financial Records Invoices, payments, expenses, orders Accounting and reporting features

⚙ How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core accounting and invoice management features
  • Sync your business data securely across your devices
  • Enable the customer portal for your customers to view their invoices and ledger
  • Send low-stock alerts and payment reminders within the app
  • Generate PDF invoices and financial reports
  • Authenticate users (admin, staff, and customer logins)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data or your customers’ data to any third party for marketing purposes.

💾 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in two locations:

  • On your device (local): All business data, invoices, and customer records are stored in a local SQLite database on your Android device. This data remains on your device and is accessible even without an internet connection.
  • Cloud (Supabase): When you choose to sync, data is encrypted and transmitted to Supabase cloud servers (hosted on AWS infrastructure). Supabase is compliant with industry-standard security practices including data encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL).

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

🔒 App Permissions

DukanBuddy requests the following Android permissions:

Permission Why it is needed
INTERNET Cloud sync, Supabase authentication, and customer portal
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Detect online/offline status to show connectivity warnings
CAMERA Capture photos of purchase bills to attach to purchase records

Biometric data (fingerprint/face) is processed entirely on your device by Android’s secure hardware. We never access, store, or transmit your biometric data.

🔗 Third-Party Services

DukanBuddy uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – Cloud database, authentication, and real-time sync. Data processed by Supabase is subject to the Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts – Font rendering. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Google AdMob – In-app advertising. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data subject to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Flutter / Google – App framework. Crash reporting may be collected by the underlying Flutter engine.

🛍 Customer Portal

If you enable the Customer Portal, your customers can log in using their phone number and a portal password set by you. Their data (invoices, ledger, order history) is tied to their customer account in your business database.

As the business owner, you are responsible for informing your customers about how their data is stored and used within DukanBuddy. You should obtain appropriate consent from your customers before entering their data into the app.

📂 Data Retention & Deletion

Your business data is retained on your device for as long as you use the app. Cloud-synced data is retained on Supabase servers until you request deletion.

To delete your data:

  • Local data: Use the “Factory Reset” option in the app’s Settings screen to permanently clear all local data.
  • Cloud data & account deletion: Visit our Account Deletion page or contact us at the email below. We will process requests within 30 days.

👶 Children’s Privacy

DukanBuddy is designed for business use and is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
