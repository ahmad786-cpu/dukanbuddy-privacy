⚠ Before You Delete

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your business data including invoices, customers, stock, and financial records will be permanently deleted.

Please make sure you have exported or backed up any important data before proceeding.

📱 Option 1 – Delete Data from the App

You can clear all your local data directly from within the app:

  1. Open DukanBuddy on your device
  2. Go to Settings (bottom navigation bar)
  3. Scroll down to Danger Zone
  4. Tap “Factory Reset”
  5. Confirm the deletion – all local data will be permanently erased

This removes all data stored on your device immediately.

☁ Option 2 – Delete Cloud Account & Data

To delete your Supabase cloud account and all associated cloud-stored data, send us a deletion request by email:

  1. Send an email to af6781114@gmail.com
  2. Subject: Account Deletion Request – DukanBuddy
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days
